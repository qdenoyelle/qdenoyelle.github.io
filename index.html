Short Bio

Since September 2018, I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Biomedical Imaging Group (BIG) at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), working with Pr. Michael Unser. In July 2018, I have completed a PhD in applied mathematics at Université Paris-Dauphine (CEREMADE) and École Normale Supérieure (DMA). My advisors were Gabriel Peyré and Vincent Duval.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • continuous sparse inverse problems,
  • continuous optimization algorithms (check my GitHub page),
  • applications to machine learning and signal processing.
