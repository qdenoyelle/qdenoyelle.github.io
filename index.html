Short Bio

Since September 2020, I am Maître de Conférence in the MAP5 Laboratory at University Paris Descartes. Before, I was a post-doctoral researcher in the Biomedical Imaging Group (BIG) of Michael Unser at EPFL. In 2018, I defended my PhD, done under the supervision of Gabriel Peyré and Vincent Duval, in applied mathematics at University Paris-Dauphine (CEREMADE).

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • sparse inverse problems,
  • convex optimization algorithms (check my GitHub page),
  • applications to machine learning, signal and image processing.
